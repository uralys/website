Clic on the screen to use commands !
You need a Sun Java2 JRE1.5.0 to play
if any problem write to dugne@enseirb.fr
Moves
A-D <=> left-right
W <=> jump
Shoots
left clic <=> shoot
right clic <=> change blub type
moving mouse <=> aim for red blubs
Camera
Directional keys <=> moving camera
K <=> change the follow-up of the camera
